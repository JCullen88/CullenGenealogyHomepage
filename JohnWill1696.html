Will of John Cullen, Yeoman of Upton
Written Jun 8, 1696
Jim Cullen

John Cullen, Yeoman of Upton, mentions several family members including his wife Frances and four children; Frances, Mary, and Gervas. John was buried June 14, 1696, at St Peter & St Paul's Church in Upton.


The Will of John Cullen, Yeoman of Upton, Nottinghamshire
Written Jun 8, 1696


In the name of God Amen I John Cullen of Upton in the county of Nott yeom doe make my last Will & Testamt in maner & form following That is to say ffirst my Will is that the Oxgang of land wch formerly I settled upon my Daughtr Frances Barrett shall still remain & abide upon her and her heirs for ever And as for my personall estate I give & bequeath as followeth ffirst I give unto Frances my dear Wife ffive shillings of lawfull money of England to be paid within one year next after my diseas Item I give unto Mary Cullen my daughtr in Law one shilling to be paid as aforesaid Item I give unto her three Children Mary Cullen Gervas Cullen and Francis Cullen one shilling apiece to be paid to them as aforesaid And all the rest of my psonall estate I give unto Gervas Cullen my son of whom I make Sole Executrix of this my last Will & Testamt after my debts & funerall charges be paid In wittens whereof I have hereunto put my hand & Seale this 8th day of June Anno Dom 1696 John Cullen Sealed & published in the psence of John Cullen X his mark George Cullin O his mark Tho: Cullin Wm Barrett.



The will does not contain much in the way of fields or other property but there are some very useful family details. More notes will be added here as we learn more.
