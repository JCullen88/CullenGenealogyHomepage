Birth Registers: Surname Cullen
Manorhamilton, Co. Leitrim
Jim Bruen and daughter Pam
Jim Bruen, a descendant of the Cullen family in Co. Leitrim, has forwarded this page of birth registers for the Cullens of Manorhamilton. It was extracted from the microfilmed records available at the LDS Family History Centers. Jim's daughter Pam performed the actual data entry as part of a WPA project. Updates will be made as the individuals in the table below are identified. I hope then to have more accurate dates and parent's names available on this page. You may also want to take a look at the birth and christening data from Civil Registration in Co's Leitrim and Roscommon. These can be found in the Records Archive.
