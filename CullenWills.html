Cullen Will Transcriptions
Upton, Nottinghamshire
Jim Cullen

This page is in progress and is an effort to reorganize access to will transcriptions for Cullens in Upton, Nottinghamshire.



  • Richard Cullen (1580): Husbandman of Upton c.1540-1580. Long recognized as the earliest member of the Cullen Family in Upton, Nottinghamshire.
  • Thomas Cullen (1628): Yeoman and Churchwarden of Upton from 1600-1625. Widely believed to be the son of Richard Cullen above.
  • Susanna Cullen (1652): The wife of Thomas Cullen, Yeoman of Upton (c1573-1628). They were married in November of 1611 in Upton. There's lots of family detail in this will.
  • John Cullen (1696): Yeoman of Upton.
  • Gervase Cullen (1708): Yeoman of Upton and husband to Theodosia. A complex will with incredible holdings of real estate, indicating a high status in Upton.
  • Thomas Cullen (1715): Yeoman of Upton c.1652-1715. His family connection to the Cullens of Upton is not known for certain but we have some very good clues!
  • Gervase Cullen (1722): Yeoman of Upton. He was the nephew of Gervase (d.1708) & Theodosia Cullen in the will listed above.
