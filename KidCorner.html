Fun & Games for our little visitors!


Welcome to Kid's Corner, a special area of the Cullen Genealogy Homepage for all of our little Cullen Family members. While the grownups research record transcripts for genealogical information, the young ones can color pictures or do other fun activities and not be left out of the fun! Maybe they will in later years remember and take an interest in their family history. Maybe one of the children in your family will be the next Cullen Family historian. Maybe... maybe not... in either case, even the littlest Cullens deserve their very own place on this site. I hope they enjoy what I've put here for them. All content rigorously tested by my own children, Tori & Brandon Cullen.

The Coat of Arms of the Irish O'Cullen family in Leinster, the origin of a great many Cullen families in the world today. This sept is said to be of very ancient lineage, descended from the great heroes and warriors of legend.
Cullenstown Castle in Co Wexford, Ireland. Perhaps the location where the Cullen Family was able to shine its brightest. The families connected to Cullenstown Castle have produced some of the best known Cullens, including Fr Paul Cullen, the first Irish Cardinal.
Many Cullen families descended from the yeoman farmers of the English Midlands can claim the distinction of having ancestors who had close connections to St Peter's at Upton, Nottinghamshire for nearly half a millenium. The church was built about the year 1250.
