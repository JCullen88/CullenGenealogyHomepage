Protestation Return
Southwell, Nottinghamshire 1642
Jim Cullen

This is a transcription of the Protestation Return for Southwell, Nottinghamshire dated March 7, 1641 (1642 on the new calendar). There are 290+ names on this list. The population of Southwell at the time was between 1000 and 1300 persons and males 18 years and older make up about 30% of the total. Again, there are no Cullens on this Protestation Return.
