Jim Cullen and family members in the United Kingdom
Cullen Families of Nottinghamshire Part2 (K-Z): These tables are by no means complete and represent an attempt to integrate several sets of source data into one collection arranged by family. They are the result of ongoing research in the indicated areas and are being posted as they become available. I will be working myself to put all the information I have available into the family groups but please consider these pages a community workspace. Anyone can make a contribution or discover a missing connection. I'm sure there is even information from other areas of this site that may have been overlooked. Feel free to forward any information or clarification for inclusion on this page.

Wife: Jane
Marriage: Upton?
Children:
none known--Upton
Notes: This William Cullen is practicaly unknown in the records. The only note regards a William Cullen, constable of Upton in 1645, and a mention of his wife Jane. Four Cullens were constables between 1640 and 1666; William Cullen in 1645, Gervaise Cullen in 1653, John Cullen in 1659, and Richard Cullen in 1666. There is the possibility that the marriage to Jane may not have lasted very long - William then remarrying shortly thereafter, possibly (though not likely) to the Katheren below.
