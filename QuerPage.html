If you would like to post a query pertaining to Cullen genealogy, there is an e-mail link provided on each page specially formatted for that purpose. Be sure to include relevant names, dates, and locations, when known. Each posting must be accompanied by an e-mail address where you can be reached should someone be able to answer your query. For your protection, all e-mail addresses are masked against robots. Due to the large amount of e-mail I receive, my mail is purged on a monthly basis. I try to reply to all messages but sometimes a message will slip through and be deleted before I have responded to it. In this case, don't give up - remind me! This is one of the reasons I recommend making a post - the posting pages are backed up on floppy disk weekly.

You are also invited to post any useful information pertaining to Cullen genealogy; send whatever you think would be useful via e-mail. Your privacy (name and e-mail address) will be honored in this case if you so desire.

To access the page for your Irish county of interest, click the name on the map below. English and Scottish page links are below that. The e-mail addresses on all of the postings have been masked. To e-mail anyone on the list, replace the *** with the @ sign when typing the address into your e-mail program. Text in red are items posted by myself. E-mail me for more information.

There are four other sites that you should definitely consider posting to:

Local Ireland: Cullen postings in the Local Names section
