Probate Inventory of Richard Cullen
Husbandman of Upton 1580
Jim Cullen

Probate will of Richard Cullen, husbandman, of Upton, Nottinghamshire. The exact date of the original document can not be made out clearly due to the age and the wrinkling of the parchment which can not be remedied due to the high risk of further damage. The month February is very clear though. According to the abstract, the date was February 19, 1579 on the old calendar - or 1580 on the new calendar.


The Probate Inventory of Richard Cullen, Husbandman of Upton, Nottinghamshire


The inventory of all the goods and cattells of Richard Cullen of Upton movable and unmovable praysed the [19'th] day of February by Thomas Barth[??] [illegible name wrinkled] John Plowman George Bykarke.

Inprimis his purse and girdell and his apparell ... 26s 8d
Item 7 Coverledes and one bed hillinge ... 53s 8d
Item 3 Mattriss and a Quilte ... 46s
Item 3 Bowlsters and twelve pillowes ... 26s 8d
Item 6 payre of lynne[n] shetes and one lynne[n] shete ... 36s
Item 5 payre of hempen sheetes ... 23s 4d
Item 14 payre of harden shetes ... 26s 8d
Item ten lynnen bo[r]de clothes and one of hempen ... 6s
Item 5 towelles and one of them of Dyaper ... 10s
Item 8 table napkynes ... 2s 8d
Item 12 pillowe beares ... 12s
Item 17 pewter chargers ... 22s 8d
Item 18 pewter [D]oublers ... 12s
Item 7 pewter saucers 8 pewter poddingers tene pewter saltes tene pewter pottes ... 10s
Item one chafen dishe 3 candelstyckes and a basone ... 6s
Item four brasse pottes a brasen morter and a pestell ... 30s
Item thr[ey] brasse pannes 3 kettelles of brasse ... 46s 8d
Item one arke with a presse ... 6s 8d
Item 2 bede steades tow lyttell formes a [trowgh] with a [covering] ... 11s
Item 3 cobbord[s] ... 26s 8d
Item 5 Arkes and a copher ... 26s
Item a py?he potte and a ?rym?? ... 2s
