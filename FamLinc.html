Cullen Families
Lincolnshire, England
compiled from the IGI and family histories
This data will be reformatted, expanded on, and a list of abbreviations will be provided at the bottom for the parish names. Posted now as a document in progress.
This data was used as a guide to the family groups and their locations while researching my own Cullen family though others have found it useful as well. Note that the dates are given as the year only.
If you are researching other surnames in Lincolnshire, take the time to visit Nan Clark's indexed surname pages for Lincolnshire Genealogical Research .
