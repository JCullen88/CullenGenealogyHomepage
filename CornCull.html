Descendants of Cornelius Cullen
& Mary Ann Rogers
Co Cavan, Ireland and New South Wales
 initial family line from various Internet sources
Cornelius Cullen was born in Co Cavan, Ireland in about 1785. The family relocated to New South Wales, having arrived in Australia onboard the "Isabella" on March 9,1822. The descendants outlined below lived mainly in the areas of Camden, Picton, and Campbelltown in New South Wales.

As with other well documented families, insightful relatives have taken up genealogy as a hobby and took the venture far enough to be able to write an entire book. The descendants of Cornelius Cullen are one such fortunate family! In 1987 a book was published by one of the family members. As more information is received, watch for this page to be updated.
