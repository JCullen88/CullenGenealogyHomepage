From: willieandliz***netscapeonline.co.uk
I am trying to trace my Irish Cullen Ancestors who appear to have originated in County Armagh. The family were all builders mainly in stone and built Cathedrals and large buildings. I have the death certificate of my Great Great Grandfather Hugh Cullen who died while working on a contract in Glasgow in 1878 he then being 68 years of age he would have been born in 1810. He was the son of James Cullen Mason (Master) and Rose Haughey of Armagh. Hugh Cullen married Mary Ann McGurk and they had seven sons and five daughters. I have traced only three children sons James and Pierce and daughter Rose.
James Cullen married Mary Ann McCann in Armagh on September 10, 1865 and had two children in Ireland Alice 1866 and Hugh 1867 they then went to Glasgow Scotland were they had James 1872, Rose 1873 and Pearce 1875.
Pierce Cullen married Catherine Corway in Dublin 1892 and all their children were born in Dublin William Francis (My Grandfather) 1893 Maud 1895, Kathleen 1897, Rose May 1899, Pearce 1903 and Sarah Josephine 1906. The family moved to Glasgow Scotland about 1911. Rose Cullen married a man named Watters in Dublin and lived in Glasgow Scotland for a time but moved on.
I believe that at least two of Hugh Cullens sons went to the USA were they worked as builders and some went to England. I believe that the daughters moved to the Dublin area and married into the Flood and Rice families. My Grandfather William Francis Cullen was born at 29 Lower Gloucester Street, Dublin. Any help would be much appreciated.
