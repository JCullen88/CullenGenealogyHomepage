Cullen Families of
Nottinghamshire
Jim Cullen and family members in the United Kingdom
These tables are by no means complete. They are the result of ongoing research in the indicated areas and are being posted as they become available. The first pass through will be to integrate my main set of notes on these families with another set of notebooks with further details, not yet connected in full. Some details will simply be entered into the 'Notes' section of a given family untill the second pass through the writing of this page. Later, as burial, christening, marriage, and other records and notes are fully connected, they will be included here as well.

Gervase20Nov1660Upton
Notes: "John Culling of Upton (and) Wilson's daughter of Westrop married at N.Muskham by Dawson Vic." dated about 26Aug1641. Vicar Wilson was buried 25Apr1628 at Upton. In Upton we find records for the burial of four daughters of John/Frances Cullen; Sarah on 20Jan1655, Eliz on 23Jul1666, Mary on 22Jun1670, and another Sarah on 24Mar1680. This makes it likely that (female) Cullen born 03Oct1656 may have been the second Sarah.
