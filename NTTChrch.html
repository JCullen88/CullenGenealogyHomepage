There are many old parish churches in the midlands of Nottinghamshire where parish register entries regarding the christenings, marriages, or burials of our Cullen families can be found. Thanks to the efforts of friends overseas, I am able to provide this photo gallery of some of the relevant parish churches in Nottinghamshire. There are links throughout the site that point to this page so the photos are arranged in no particular order.
