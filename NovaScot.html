Family of Jean and Johanna (Jeanne) JeanPerrin
Husband: Jean Urban Jeanperrin born 1720 France. Second wife was Judith Jollimois, married 1790. First name also John; middle name was also spelled Urbain; last name on occasion was also spelled Jeanparain, Janperrin, Perrine, and Perin. He was known in Lunenburg as John Perrin.

Wife: Johanna Jeanne born 1723 died 1789 Lunenburg, Nova Scotia.

Married: Unknown
Children:

  • Jean George: b. September 6,1754 Lunenburg, N. S.
    m. Catherine Elizabeth Jollimois (1758 - Nov 11,1850) on Oct 22, 1782.
  • Jean Christopher: b. August 22,1756 Lunenburg, N. S.
    m. Mary Catherine Dauphinee (1759 - Dec 30,1835) on Oct 26, 1779.

Family of Seth and Susannah (Perrin) Cullen
Husband: Seth Cullen b. June 21, 1819. Came to the US in 1840 with his brother Enos and an unknown relation 'Kate'. Was a lifelong minister of the Methodist Episcopal faith. Died Aug 25, 1907 in Paulding, Paulding Co., Ohio. Buried in Live Oak Cemetary. Obit. Thanks to Mrs. Edward Scherer, photos of Seth and his family are still in existence. Photo page is 123KB in size; expect half a minute for page to download at 28.8Kbps. See also the photo of the unknown Cullen relative, the "Lady of Nottingham".

Wife: Susannah Perrin b. Dec 01, 1826 to David and Elizabeth (Langille) Perrin in River John, Pictou, Nova Scotia. Died Dec 16, 1905. Buried in Live Oak Cemetary. Obit. Susan's family in Nova Scotia is well known and many genealogies of the various related families have been researched. This page has been devoted to Susannah's Nova Scotia ancestors.
