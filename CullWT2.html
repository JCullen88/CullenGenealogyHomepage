Part Two: Goods and Chattels of
Thomas Cullen 1652-1715
James C. Cullen Jr., Cliff Cullen, Jim Cullen

The Goods and Chattels of Thomas Cullen. Some of the items mentioned are still not known but work continues. Note the appearance of Gervas Cullen, brother of Thomas and one of the Executors of the will of Thomas Cullen.
