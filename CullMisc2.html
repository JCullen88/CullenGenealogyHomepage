Cullens registered as Freemasons: Manorhamilton

April 4, 1782: Pat Cullen was registered as a founder member of Lodge No. 600, Manorhamilton.

February 8, 1811: Hu. Cullen was registered at Lodge No. 600, Manorhamilton. The Lodge warrant was cancelled in 1833.

August 11, 1858: Edmund Willoughby Cullen was registered in Lodge No. 187 in Drumkeerin, which moved to Manorhamilton in 1865. Three more Cullens registered into Lodge No. 187 after the move to Manorhamilton:
  • May 20, 1865: John Evans Cullen registered into Lodge 187 from Lodge No. 129, in Belleek, Co Fermanagh.


  • October 3, 1867: Henry C.C.Cullen and Francis J. Cullen registered in Lodge 187.

January 9, 1862: John James Marcus Cullen registered in Lodge 129 in Belleek, Co Fermanagh.

March 16, 1862: John Evans Cullen initiated into Lodge No. 129 in Belleek, Fermanagh.
