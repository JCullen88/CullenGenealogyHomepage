Various emigrants from Ireland & England to the USA
  • Richard Cullen, on the ship New World, left Liverpool on March 19th 1855, bound for New York.
  • Alice Cullan, on the ship Infantry, left Liverpool on July 29th 1850, bound for New York.
  • John Cullin from Kilkenny, sailed on the ship Huntress, from Dublin to New York, arriving on June 24th 1811. The ship's Captain was Thomas Ronson.
  • Mary Cullen, on the ship St. Patrick, from Liverpool to New York on September 3rd 1849.
  • Francis Cullin, 16 years of age, a labourer from Resinuer Co. Leitrim, left from Ballyshannon, May 10th 1804, bound for New Castle & Philadelphia. He sailed on the Brig Jefferson of Newberry Port, USA. The Captain was Danial Knight.

These concern events in the Dioceses of Kildare and Leighlin, Co. Carlow.
