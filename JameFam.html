4'th Generation: Enos and Betsy Jane (James) Cullen
Husband: Enos Cullen ch. Apr 8, 1821 Great Hale, Lincs. Came to the US in 1840 with his brother Seth and an unknown relative 'Kate'. Died Jan 18, 1870 Sandusky(?) Co., Ohio. Buried in Castalia Cemetary, Erie Co., Ohio. Only one family photo is known to have survived.

Wife: Betsy Jane James b. Apr 18, 1838 Bloomingville, Erie Co., Ohio to Henry and Abbigail (Hitchcock) James. Died Jul 28, 1918 Castalia, Erie Co., Ohio. Buried in Castalia Cemetary next to Enos but there is no stone there for her. Obit.

Married: abt. 1856 Erie(?) Co., Ohio.
Children:

  • Helen: b. May 25, 1859 Erie Co., Ohio. Obit.
    m. George H. Pelton Apr 12, 1880 Sandusky Co., Ohio. Obit.
  • Charles E. : b. Feb 27, 1861 Maumee, Lucas Co., Ohio.
    m. Sarah E. Goodwin Jul 2, 1881 Sandusky Co., Ohio.
  • John W. : b.Oct 18, 1863 Bloomingville, Erie Co., Ohio.
    m. Alice Jane Coonrod Dec 24, 1884 Sandusky Co., Ohio.
