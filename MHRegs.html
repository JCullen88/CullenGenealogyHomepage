Parish Registers: Surname Cullen
Co. Leitrim, Ireland
IGI and other sources
The parish registers for Ireland are somewhat limited but can be helpful, especially for someone just starting out in their search for relatives around the world. The IGI is not the ultimate authority, nor is it the only source of family information available. It's best use is as a guide to further research. Records from around the world are available together in one place, allowing immediate access to records covering many time periods and locations. The event codes below are: (first letter) F=female, M=male, H=husband, W=wife: (second letter) B=birth, C=christening, M=marriage, D=death. See also the Civil Registration records for Co's Leitrim and Roscommon in the Records Archive.
