I am seeking any information on the following: Daniel Patrick CULLEN born about 1871 ? may have been originally from County Clare Ireland and died 1937 or 1938 in Australia. He was probably Catholic but probably not a practising one. I'm unsure of any Parish attachment. He may have lived in Bangor near Belfast in Brians Burn Gardens and had two sisters Annie and Amy Cullen. He Married on 24/9/1902 in Ireland to Mary Elizabeth McGIBBON born ? who may have been originally from County Armagh Ireland and died 1940 ? in Australia. She had another relative named BRADY who was in the India Corps. They had seven surviving children 6 boys one girl emigrated to Australia in 1909? He was school teacher. Two children also born in Ireland died quite young. I have information on each of their children who are now all deceased except my father.
