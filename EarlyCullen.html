Early Cullens of Upton Nottinghamshire
Miscellaneous Records Prior to 1600
Jim Cullen

This page will hold the miscellaneous records I've come across that demonstrate that there were Cullen families living in and around Upton prior to about 1600. These references will be little more than scraps with minimal information given to go along with them. These families, unknown until now, were contemporaries of Richard Cullen who died in Upton in 1580. While connections to the Cullens of Upton and surrounding areas are not known, it can be assumed there is some connection in some cases. There is, for example, known records of very early Cullens in neighboring Lincolnshire but I don't mention them much on this site since there is just no evidence yet for their connection to the known families of Cullen in the areas around Upton, Nottinghamshire.
